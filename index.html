"Weather Tips & Safety"

Stay safe and informed with these seasonal weather safety tips. Whether it's monsoon, summer, or winter — be prepared and stay healthy.

Monsoon Safety Tips

  • Carry an umbrella or raincoat.
  • Wear non-slippery shoes.
  • Avoid flood water paths.
  • Waterproof your gadgets.
  • Check weather alerts.

Summer Heat Care

  • Stay hydrated with water & fruits.
  • Avoid sun between 12-3 PM.
  • Wear light, cotton clothes.
  • Use sunscreen for UV protection.
  • Keep indoors cool & airy.

Air Quality Tips

  • Check AQI before outdoor plans.
  • Use N95 mask in pollution.
  • Air purifiers for indoor use.
  • Avoid morning walks in smog.
  • Stay indoors on poor AQI days.

Winter Protection

  • Dress in warm layers.
  • Cover head, hands, and feet.
  • Keep skin moisturized.
  • Stay active to generate heat.
  • Drink warm fluids.
